About Me

Hello!

I am an environmental professional focused on GIS, remote sensing, and field-based ecological monitoring. My work combines spatial analysis and on-the-ground data collection to support restoration planning, habitat management, and practical environmental decision-making across Southern California ecosystems.

I enjoy translating complex environmental data into clear maps and usable insights for planners, scientists, and conservation teams. Outside of my technical work, I am a hobbyist photographer with a strong interest in nature and landscape imagery, which complements how I observe and interpret ecosystems in the field.
